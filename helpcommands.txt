```Weather Bot Commands:
 !help : displays this help menu.
 
 !current <city> : displays today's weather based on the user-inputted city (default: Farhenheit).
    - !current -F <city> : displays today's weather in Farhenheit.
    - !current -C <city> : displays today's weather in Celsius.

 !weekly <city> : display's the weekly outlook on weather (default: Farhenheit).
    - !weekly -F <city> : displays weekly weather in Farhenheit.
    - !weekly -C <city> : displays weekly weather in Celsius.
    ```
